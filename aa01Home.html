About

“

Surgical Skateboarding is one of Melbourne's largest online communities of qualified surgeons who share a passion for skateboarding. Founded in March 1999, Surgical Skateboarding provides a place for members to meet, arrange skating/professional networking events, and purchase bespoke merchandise that is tailored to their lifestyle.

We have over 200 members, and connections to other medical-skate groups across Australia! We accept members of all skill levels, so if you're new to skating or just beginning your undergraduate medical degree, feel free to come along to a meetup or networking event. Existing members are always there to help give newbies some pointers, after all, it's not brain surgery - always...

”
